What personal data we collect and why we collect it

When you sign up for an account, we collect your email address and a password of your creation. This allows us to identify you when you log into our platform, so that we can display details specific to your account. This include video playlists and battle score cards.

Who we share your data with

We don't share your data with anyone at this time. If that changes, this policy will be updated and you will be notified via an email.

How long we retain your data

If you leave a comment, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This is so we can recognize and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue.

What rights you have over your data

If you have an account on this app, or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.
